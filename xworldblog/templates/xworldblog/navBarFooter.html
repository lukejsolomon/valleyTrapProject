Welcome to the Valley Trap Range/Fremont Isaac Walton League. One of the premier trap ranges in the midwest, the club features eight traps and a practice trap, great backgrounds, good targets, friendly people and a nice clubhouse. The range also has one of the largest high school teams in Nebraska with about 100 junior high and high school shooters on the Fremont team.
